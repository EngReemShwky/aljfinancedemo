Welcome to Jameel Finance Egypt

Jameel Finance Egypt is a subsidiary company of Abdul Latif Jameel. This association brings to Jameel Finance Egypt the business benefits of more than 75 years of deep roots, diversified businesses, broad and comprehensive expertise, Abdul Latif Jameel has been operating in Egypt through several companies.

Jameel Finance started its operations in 1999 through the United International Trading Company and successfully expanded its business in the Egyptian market.

In December 2020, Jameel Finance Egypt has obtained its consumer finance license from FRA.
